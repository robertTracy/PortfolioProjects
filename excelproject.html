I used to work for a food delivery service and I always wondered how much some of my customers made for a living. Sometimes my best tips came from the lower income neighborhoods and poor tips came from affluent neighborhoods. I got the opportunity to look at some data from a food delivery service in South America so I used it to learn some key factors in the food delivery demographics. That person may have had a bad experience and stopped using the service. More investigation might be warranted. Every customer is important.
